Industry Workers Safety Monitoring System using Smart Helmet

The SafeX system provides real-time oversight of industrial personnel working in hazardous environments. Utilizing specialized smart helmets equipped with biometric, environmental, and spatial sensors, operations centers can proactively prevent accidents through predictive AI and immediate condition alerts.

Environmental Analysis

Continuous detection of CH₄, CO, and oxygen depletion. Automated warning triggers prevent toxic exposure.

Biometric Telemetry

Heart rate, body temperature, and SpO2 tracking to ensure workers are physically healthy and capable.

Fatigue AI

Machine learning analyzes micro-movements and resting vitals to predict cognitive fatigue and exhaustion.

Spatial Tracking & SOS

Mesh-networked positioning prevents personnel getting lost. Instant SOS tactile buttons on the helmet override all communication.
